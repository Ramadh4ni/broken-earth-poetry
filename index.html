Three elegies for a world unraveling

Breath of the Forests

Broken Earth · I

Some creatures once lived here,
speaking softly through roots and rain,
where wild trees swayed with the wind
and rivers carried the sky within their gentle currents,
while the earth breathed quietly beneath endless fields of life.
✦
Then came the endless neat rows beneath a burning sun,
replacing forests with ashes and smoke,
until the rivers forgot the shape of their own paths,
and the rain began to fall like grief upon the land,
heavy enough for the wounded earth to no longer hold.

Whisper of Rain

Broken Earth · II

The rain arrives in unfamiliar months,
crossing confused seasons and restless winds,
while shifting clouds and warming skies
reshape the rhythm of the earth.
✦
Now the fields wait beneath uncertain sunlight,
the rivers rise too early, the heat lingers too long,
and every falling storm carries the quiet warning
of a world nature no longer fully remembers.
hover each image to reveal its name

Fading Tide

Broken Earth · III

The sea once sang through silver waves,
touching soft sand along the quiet beach,
while bright corals swayed beneath the clear water
and small fish danced beside the rising tide.
✦
Now broken bottles drift across the dark shore,
empty shells lie beneath polluted waves,
and the wounded ocean carries growing silence
as fewer fish return to the sea.
